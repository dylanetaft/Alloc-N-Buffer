◆ ANB_Blob_t

typedef struct ANB_Blob ANB_Blob_t

Opaque contiguous byte buffer with externally managed contents.

Unlike ANB_Slab, this is a raw byte buffer with no item tracking, alignment padding, or queue semantics. The caller manages what goes into the buffer and at what offsets.
